# SpeakRise — Hackathon Brief

## The Big Problem

Professionals across India and the developing world face two invisible ceilings:

1. **They can't speak English fluently** — They read it, write it, pass exams in it, but freeze up the moment they need to speak in a meeting, interview, or presentation. 700 million people are learning English globally, yet there's almost no affordable way to practice *speaking* daily with real feedback.

2. **They can't articulate business thinking clearly** — Even fluent speakers struggle to structure arguments, persuade stakeholders, handle objections, or tell a compelling story. Business communication is a skill nobody teaches outside of expensive MBA programs or executive coaching.

The result: talented people get overlooked for promotions, lose deals, and underperform in roles they're technically qualified for — because they can't communicate effectively.

## The Opportunity

A daily 5-minute AI conversation coach that solves both problems at once. Not a language app. Not a chatbot. A voice-first speaking partner that listens, responds naturally, and tells you exactly what to improve — every single day.

## Problems We Want the Team to Solve

### Problem 1: Language Speaking Skills

Most English learners plateau at "functional but uncomfortable." They need:

- **Daily speaking practice** — Not reading, not listening, not multiple choice. Actually speaking out loud and hearing themselves improve.
- **Real-time natural conversation** — Not scripted drills. Open-ended topics that force them to think on their feet, like a real meeting or interview would.
- **Honest, specific feedback** — Not "good job!" Generic encouragement doesn't help. Users need to know: "Your grammar was good, but your sentences are all the same length. Here's how to vary them." With scores they can track over time.
- **Targeted exercises** — If someone said "I goed to the store," don't just mark it wrong. Give them the corrected sentence to repeat. Build their vocabulary with words they almost used but didn't know.
- **Measurable progression** — A clear leveling system so users can see themselves improving week over week. From basic comprehension all the way to confident, articulate speech.

**The 5 Levels:**
1. **Learning** — Understanding words and sentences
2. **Speaking** — Using words to state basic facts
3. **Communicating** — Conveying complex ideas clearly
4. **Persuading** — Convincing through logic and emotion
5. **Inspiring** — Expert-level communication that moves people

### Problem 2: Business Understanding & Communication

Language fluency is necessary but not sufficient. Professionals also need to:

- **Structure their thinking** — Organize ideas logically before speaking. Lead with the conclusion. Support with evidence. Most people ramble because they haven't learned to structure.
- **Persuade and influence** — Use rhetoric effectively: establish credibility (ethos), connect emotionally (pathos), argue logically (logos). These aren't innate talents — they're learnable frameworks.
- **Tell stories** — The best communicators use narratives, analogies, and humor to make complex ideas stick. Business storytelling is a skill that can be practiced and scored.
- **Handle objections** — Anticipate pushback, pre-empt concerns, and respond to challenges without getting flustered. Critical for sales, fundraising, and leadership.
- **Deliver with impact** — Pacing, hooks, building curiosity, the "aha" moment. The difference between a forgettable update and a presentation people remember.

**The key insight:** You can evaluate and coach these skills through conversation. An AI that asks the right questions can assess whether someone structures their argument well, uses evidence, tells stories, or just lists facts. And it can give them specific feedback to improve.

### Problem 3: The Habit Problem

The best tool is useless if people don't use it daily. We need to solve for:

- **Low commitment, high frequency** — 5 minutes is short enough to do every day. No scheduling, no preparation, just open and talk.
- **AI speaks first** — Many learners are anxious about starting. The AI should break the ice, set the tone, and make it feel like talking to a friendly colleague.
- **Zero friction interaction** — No buttons to hold, no "press to record." Voice auto-detection so the conversation flows naturally.
- **Progress visibility** — Daily scores, skill breakdowns, and streak tracking so users can see the compound effect of daily practice.
- **Team accountability (B2B)** — In corporate settings, visibility into who's practicing and who isn't. Nudges from teammates. Leaderboards that create positive peer pressure.

## What Success Looks Like

A working product where someone can:
- [ ] Sign up and have their first AI conversation within 60 seconds
- [ ] Complete a baseline assessment that accurately gauges their level
- [ ] Do a daily 5-minute session that feels like a real conversation (not a drill)
- [ ] Receive a post-session report with honest, actionable feedback
- [ ] See skill scores for both language AND business communication dimensions
- [ ] Track measurable improvement over multiple sessions
- [ ] Get exercises that directly address their weaknesses

## Stretch Goals

- [ ] Scenario modes — practice for specific situations (job interview, sales pitch, board presentation, team standup)
- [ ] Multiple AI personas — casual friend, tough interviewer, skeptical investor, supportive mentor
- [ ] Team dashboards — aggregate view for managers to see team communication health
- [ ] Nudge system — teammates can remind each other to complete daily practice
- [ ] Leaderboards — best team, most improved, longest streak
- [ ] Industry-specific vocabulary and scenarios (tech, finance, healthcare, sales)

## Business Context

**Market:** Global English learning market is $60B+ and growing. Corporate communication training is a $15B+ segment. Conversation practice is the most underserved area in both.

**Monetization:**
- **Free:** 1 session/day — enough to build the habit
- **Premium ($9.99/mo):** Unlimited sessions, advanced analytics, scenario modes
- **B2B ($X/seat/mo):** Team dashboards, nudges, manager insights, org-level communication analytics

**Differentiation vs. Duolingo, ELSA, etc.:**
- Voice-first, not text-based — you actually speak
- Business communication, not just language — rhetoric, storytelling, persuasion
- AI speaks first — reduces anxiety, sets the tone
- Zero-button interaction — feels like a real conversation
- Actionable feedback with real exercises — not just a score
- 5-minute daily habit — low commitment, high retention
